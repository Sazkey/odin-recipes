Chicken Pineapple Curry with Rice

Description

Yummy Chicken Pineapple Curry Recipe with Rice that has the same feeling like when its from your local asian restaurant but even more fresh and enjoyable! :D

Ingredients (for 2 people)

  • 250 g chicken breast fillet(s)
  • 1 can pineapple pieces, approx. 340 g
  • 100 ml cream
  • 100 g cream cheese spread
  • 2 spring onions, cut into fine rings
  • 2 garlic cloves, pressed
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 3 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp chicken broth powder
  • 2 bags rice (125g)
  • some sun flower oil
  • salt and pepper
  • chili powder

Steps

  1. Cook the rice according to the instructions on the packet.

  2. Skin the meat, wash it and cut it into cube-sized pieces. Fry it in a pan with a little oil. When it starts to brown slightly, add the honey. Add the spring onions and garlic and sauté briefly. Drain the pineapple and collect the juice, then add the pineapple pieces to the pan and sauté briefly. Add the curry and fry everything briefly.

  3. Deglaze with cream. Add the processed cheese in pieces and let it melt. Bring everything to the boil, season with salt, pepper, curry, stock powder, a little ground chili and the pineapple juice and then serve on the rice.
